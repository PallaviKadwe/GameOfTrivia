Welcome to a Game of Trivia

This is a trivia on food. It is an opportunity for you to explore fun facts on food and reply to the questions presented.

Rules and scoring system

You have a total of 10 questions to answer. You can choose from four options.
The correct answer on the first attempt gives you a score of 40 points, the second 30, the third 20 and the last 0.

Each question is timed for 10 seconds. You move on to the next questions if you do not answer within the time limit.
